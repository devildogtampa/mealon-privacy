What Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account information (name, email, profile)
  • Your saved favorites
  • Your search history
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Retention Period

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes as required by law.

Subscription Cancellation

If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through Google Play before requesting account deletion.
