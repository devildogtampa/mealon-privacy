Privacy Policy for Meal On!

Last Updated: November 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Meal On! ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

1. Account Information

  • Name, email address, and password (encrypted)

2. Location Information

  • Precise GPS coordinates (with your permission)
  • Purpose: Show nearby restaurants, calculate distances, and provide driving directions
  • You can control location permissions through your device settings at any time

3. Usage Information

  • Buttons clicked and features used
  • Restaurants saved to favorites
  • Search methods
  • Purpose: Understand which features you find most useful and improve the app

4. Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Purpose: Fraud protection and technical support

5. Subscription Information

  • Subscription tier (Basic, Orange, or Blue)
  • Subscription status and changes
  • Date of signup
  • Date of subscription modifications
  • Date of account deletion (if applicable)
  • Note: Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play and App Store

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Show you nearby restaurants based on your location
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Understand which features users find most valuable

We will not sell your personal information to third parties.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics services. All usage data (button clicks, favorites, feature usage) is collected and stored internally through our own systems to improve the app. This data is never shared with third parties except our infrastructure and service providers as described below.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

Google Places API

We use Google Places API to provide restaurant information, ratings, reviews, and location data. When you search for restaurants, your location data is sent to Google according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Mapbox API

We use Mapbox for geolocation services and calculating driving directions to restaurants. Your location data is processed by Mapbox according to Mapbox's Privacy Policy.

Supabase

We use Supabase as our backend service provider for user authentication and secure data storage. Your data is encrypted and stored on their servers in accordance with industry security standards.

Google Play Billing

For in-app subscriptions on Android devices, we use Google Play's billing system. Google processes all payment information according to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not store or have access to your credit card or payment information. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status to provide you with the appropriate tier features.

Apple App Store In-App Purchases

For in-app subscriptions on iOS devices, we use Apple's In-App Purchase system. Apple processes all payment information according to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not store or have access to your credit card or payment information. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status to provide you with the appropriate tier features.

Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Supabase: Backend infrastructure and data storage
  • Google Places API: Restaurant information and location services
  • Mapbox API: Geolocation and driving directions
  • Google Play: Subscription processing

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only to provide services to us.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security issues
  • Protect the safety of our users

Business Transfers

If Meal On! is acquired, merges with another company, or sells substantially all of its assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you via in-app notification before any such transfer occurs and before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Data Retention

We will keep your personal information for as long as needed to perform our obligations to you, or for as long as legally permitted. The criteria used to determine our retention periods include:

  • The length of time we have an ongoing relationship with you (i.e., as long as your account is active)
  • Whether there is a legal obligation to which we are subject (such as tax and financial record requirements)
  • Whether retention is advisable in light of our legal position (such as in regard to applicable statutes of limitations, litigation, or regulatory investigations)

For example, we keep your account information, like your name, email address, and password, for as long as your account exists so that you may access it.

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at devildogtampa@gmail.com or by deleting your account through the app. We will process deletion requests in accordance with applicable law, except where retention is required for legal compliance.

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

You have the right to know what personal information we have collected about you and request a copy of that information.

You have the right to correct inaccurate personal information.

Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. You can delete your account at any time through the app or by contacting us.

You can control location permissions through your device settings at any time (Device Settings > Meal On! > Location). Disabling location will limit certain app features that require location data.

To exercise any of these rights:

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive data is encrypted when stored
  • Access controls: Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure infrastructure: Data stored on Supabase's secure cloud servers
  • Regular security reviews: Ongoing monitoring for vulnerabilities
  • Password protection: Your password is encrypted using industry-standard hashing

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you via in-app notification and will provide information about the breach and steps you can take to protect yourself.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our service is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Users between 13-17 should have parental or guardian consent before using our app.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, or from a user between 13-17 without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately upon discovery. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at devildogtampa@gmail.com.
